# Markup keyboard shortcuts

## Switching views

* `1` – design view (phg)
* `2` – markup view (html)
* `3` – transparent design over markup
* `4` – transparent and inverted design over markup

## Transparency

* `[` – decrease transparency
* `]` – increase transparency

## Zoom

* `-` – zoom out
* `+` – zoom in

## Sidebar

* `~` – Toggle the sidebar with the blocks list
